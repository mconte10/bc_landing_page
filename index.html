Brittany Maschal

Brittany held positions in admissions and student services at Princeton University, the University of Pennsylvania at Penn Law and The Wharton School, and the Johns Hopkins University School of Advanced International Studies (SAIS).

She received her doctorate from George Washington University, master’s from the University of Pennsylvania, and bachelor's from the University of Vermont.
She was an alumni interviewer and admissions representative for the Graduate School of Education at the University of Pennsylvania, where she was also an Executive Board Member and Membership Director of the Alumni Association.
She is a member of the Independent Educational Consultants Association and the Professional Association of Resume Writers and Career Coaches.
She has held leadership roles with IECA as both the Vice President of the Graduate Committee and Chair of the Business Roundtable.

Caitlin Andersen

Caitlin has an MBA from the University of Oxford, Saïd Business School.
She received graduate-level writing distinctions on individual essays from Harvard, Stanford, and Oxford professors. She graduated Summa Cum Laude with a Bachelor’s degree in Technical Communication from the University of Texas at San Antonio with a GPA of 4.0 / 4.0. She ranked 1 of 1668 in her class.

Caitlin has served as an MBA+ Postgraduate Strategist since 2018 and specializes in business programs.
She has advised 100+ postgrad students from 25+ countries on career strategy and US/UK/EU university admissions.
Additionally, she coaches executives in finance, tech, marketing, public policy, and management consulting (MBB, Big 4, BlackRock, Central Banks, Google, Apple, Amazon).

Recently, she was featured on GMAT Club's LIVE Youtube Channel with over 102K subscribers.
